--- title: Everyone dies. ---

So will I, and here is my will

Is it strange to have a webpage on the internet with your wishes for after your death? I think so, but I know that it can be very handy if the people around you don't agree on the way your funeral should be or how your belongings should be distributed.

I give my family full power to do whatever they want after my death (except for my donorship). If my family wants something one way and someone else (who isn't a family member) wants it another, please obey my family. They probably know best.

Cremation or burial?

Cremation. You can flush my ashes down the toilet or place them somewhere nice, I don't care.

The funny thing with cremations is that you still need a coffin. Please buy the cheapest one or build one yourself if you like that more.

As for music, I like Seasons in the Sun by Terry Jacks at the moment - it will probably change later. Don't interpret it too literally, I just like the happiness in the song. Please remember the fun times and forget the bad ones ;-)

In Holland they usually serve cake or something with coffee. Whatever floats your boat. But, please serve some special beers after or before the whole thing. You don't have to party if you don't want, but it is definitely not forbidden!

I always liked speeches, so if someone wants to give a speech, feel free to do so. I can't wait to see the tears (o wait, I'll be dead by then)!

Digital life

Because I'm kind of a digital person I have a lot online. I always make sure that someone can access my main email account. For now that is my brother Harmen: can takeover my email and turn off all services.

If there is copyright on any of my work and nobody depends on the money coming out of it, please release it into the public.

I'm fine with releasing all my personal data (photos, emails, websites, written texts, ...) into the public as well. That would be cool, but it is also okay when people don't want their data to be shared. Privacy was always very important to me, so I understand!

Belongings

I probably won't have money when I'm dead, so be careful. The rest of my property is your business. If something is worth a lot, then you can also sell it and divide up the money - as long as there is no fight about stuff. But for now that would be silly, I only have an iMac and a Ducati Monster.

As for my own body, I'm an organ donor. So please give my parts to people who can use it.
