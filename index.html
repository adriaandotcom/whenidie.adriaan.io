Everyone dies.

So will I, here is my will

Is it strange to have a webpage on the internet with your wishes after your death? I think so, but I know that it can be very handy if the people around you don't agree on the way your funeral will be or how your belongings will be distributed.

Cremation or burial?

The funny thing with cremations is that you still need a coffin. Please buy the cheapest one or build one yourself if you like that more.

As for music, I like Seasons in the Sun of Terry Jacks at the moment, it will probably change later. Don't take such a lyric personal or too literal, I just like the happiness in the song, please remember the fun times and forget the bad ones ;-)

Digital life

Because I'm kind of a digital person I have a lot online. I always make sure that someone can access my main email account. For now that is my brother Harmen, he can takeover my email and turn of all services.

If there is copyright on any of my work and nobody depends on the money coming out of it, please release it into the public.

I'm fine with releasing all my personal data (photos, emails, websites, written texts, ...) into the public as well. That would be cool, but it is also okay when people don't want their data to be shared. Privacy was always very important to me, so I will understand those people.
